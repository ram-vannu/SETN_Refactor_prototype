Sourashtra Engineers & Technologists Network

Empowering through Education, Scholarships & Career Guidance

SETN is a community platform dedicated to supporting Sourashtra engineers, technologists, and students pursuing professional degrees. We provide comprehensive educational support, scholarship opportunities, expert guidance, and career counseling to help our community members achieve their academic and professional goals.

Our Mission: To foster knowledge sharing, provide financial scholarships, offer career guidance, and create employment opportunities for students and professionals in our community.
